Weather App

When getting my daughter dressed for school, it was important for me to know how cold the day would start out as and how hot it would be at the end of school. That way she could easily be prepared. Most weather reports just show you the high and low. I needed to know for specific times of the day. I set an API call to get the weather at my daughters school location and then had it check the weather at drop off time 3 hours later and 3 hours after that around pick up time. Just had to easily go to my website and look every morning. Its not pretty, its functional.
